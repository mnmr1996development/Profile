  • NYC Crime Visualiztion

    Project Description:

    In this project, we looked at the share of crimes of each district in the city of New York. New York is a big and a very diverse city in the united states. Crime is something troubling for everyday New Yorkers. To make police deployment more effective in crime prevention, pinpointing the most crime riddled area is helpful. Therefore, in this project we analyze different crimes in the city with the corresponding areas where they are mostly committed. We made available to the user each crimes in a simple and clear visualization. We have three different type of visualizations. We are interpreting the data on a map, in a scatter plot, and in a bubble chart.The user will be able to spot the predominant type of crime in a specific district.
